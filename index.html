Overview

Lodha Sarjapur, located in Bangalore, redefines luxury living with meticulously crafted 3 & 3.5 BHK homes featuring premium finishes. Spanning a sprawling 19-acre township with 80% open space, Lodha Sarjapur boasts 8 iconic towers. At Lodha Sarjapur, residents enjoy world-class amenities, including dedicated sports courts, a state-of-the-art gym, vibrant kids’ play areas, and 24/7 security, ensuring peace of mind. Lodha Sarjapur designs every facility to boost wellness, activity, and comfort. The thriving community at Lodha Sarjapur connects like-minded individuals who cherish elegance and belonging.
